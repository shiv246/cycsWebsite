Canadian
Youth for
COVID-19
Support

Canadian Youth for COVID-19 Support is a group
of youth working towards providing individuals
with an opportunity to support local communities
and families during the pandemic. CYCS
distributes handwritten notes, masks and seeds
to neighbourhoods and takes donations to help
the frontline workers at the William Osler Health
System.

Our mission is to brighten the lives of individuals and
families who are struggling through these unprecedented
times with the involvement of youth. Engaging youth in
mental health activities throughout the course of this
pandemic will improve their livelihoods along with those of
the community members and the health care heroes they
support.

Handwritten Notes

Those living in long term care homes, especially seniors,
find it difficult to stay positive during these unprecedented
times. Without the much-needed support of friends and
family, mental health is at an all-time low. However, CYCS
hopes to reverse this pattern by providing seniors with the
support they need through the delivery of handwritten
notes.
